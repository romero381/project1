The Great Gatsby by F. Scott Fitzgerald is a novel set in the 1920s, following the life of Jay Gatsby, a wealthy but mysterious man who throws extravagant parties in hopes of reuniting with his lost love, Daisy Buchanan. The story is narrated by Nick Carraway, who moves to Long Island and becomes Gatsby's neighbor. Nick also befriends Daisy’s husband, Tom Buchanan, who is having an affair with Myrtle Wilson. Throughout the novel, Gatsby’s obsession with Daisy and his desire to reclaim the past leads to tragedy.
